This is the home page for the Morph!Tale comic/games.Morph!tale is an AU(Alternative Universe) of the game Undertale. If you haven't finished Undertale yet, I recommend you to finish the game before continuing.This AU is a timeline where Frisk leaves the Underground after a neutral route, and a new human arrives and starts a genocide route. There is a comic and fan game for the AU.
