ABOUT ME
I'm Sean Choi, currently a 7th grade student in JLS middle school.
I have a lot of interesting in creating art and coding.
I also love the game Undertale very much, and I decided to create the AU Morph!tale based on it.
I started coding with Scratch when I was in 3rd grade.
Then, I played Undertale and was so impressed that I decided to recreate it in Scratch.
However, it was near-impossible to create the system in Scratch(as I had to keep track of all files with lists and stuff)
so I moved on to scripting. First started Python, then tried Unity, then HTML and Javascript.
I'm still experimenting with a lot of stuff, but I'm currently happy with CYF, and engine made by RTL,
and I'm drawing the comic and coding the game for Morph!tale!
